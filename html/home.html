About Us

Welcome to our barangay, a thriving community dedicated to serving our residents with integrity and commitment. We strive to create a safe, inclusive, and progressive environment by providing essential public services, fostering local development, and ensuring the well-being of every citizen.

From public assistance programs to community-driven initiatives, we are here to support and uplift our people. Together, we build a stronger, more connected barangay.
